How DonationsBuild Communities

The Prophet, peace and blessings of Allah be upon him, has said: "I and the one who looks after an orphan and provides for him will be like this in Paradise," showing his middle and index fingers and separating them a little" [Bukhari]

Charity, preached by every religion of the world, is a way of bringing justice to society. A community can flourish only when its members do not spend all their wealth to fulfill their own desires but reserve a portion of it for relatives, neighbors, the poor and the needy. When we give without expecting any reward in return we in essence are funding food, clothing, shelter, education and much more that these children might have never gotten. Charity begins at home but its impact can be seen and felt in every facet of our lives. So give and encourage others and Inshallah we can try to make a better world after all.

How Can You Donate?

  • 2. Volunteer your time to teach young orphan children.
  • 3. Feed orphans and the poor.
