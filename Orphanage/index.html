Our Mission

Our work starts with empowering children and young people to become positive members of society. Our goal is to provide food, clothing and shelter to children along with a strong effort on religious, educational and moral development. Through education, religious studies and a loving home we want these at-risk girls to achieve their full potential.

October 22 2011

The Start

Establishment of My Home as a orphanage for young girls.

March 24 2012

Presentation Day

A event day was held to introduce our girls to members of the community.

December 1 2012

Institution for Islamic Education

A small institute was started for educating older girls in quran, fiq, and tafseer.
